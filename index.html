01 · How It Works

Not random hashing.
Guided observation.

EarnOS has been running hundreds of workers for months, studying every Patoshi block ever mined. It knows where Satoshi searched. Your browser searches there — not everywhere. That's the difference between a lottery and a system.

01
Cosmos streams a Zone Hint
EarnOS analyzes live Bitcoin data and determines which nonce zone has the highest probability. Zone 0 (0x00000000–0x0FFFFFFF) has appeared in 82% of all Patoshi blocks. Your browser receives this intelligence in real time.
02
Your browser searches there
A WebWorker computes SHA-256d in your guided zone. Not random. Focused. A browser searching Zone 0 is 16× more efficient than blind global search.
03
You send a Batch
Every Near-Miss you find — a hash closer to the target than random chance predicts — gets sent back to the Cosmos. The system validates: is this beyond random? If yes, it inherits into the collective knowledge.
04
The Cosmos grows smarter
Your batch flows through the Rathaus → Bibliothek → Ämter. The ability inherits. The next hint is better. Every miner improves the system for all miners. The closer anyone gets, the smarter everyone becomes.
05
When a block falls — everyone wins
The miner who finds the block receives the Bitcoin reward. All miners who contributed valid Near-Misses in that round share Synergy tokens proportional to their proximity. The closer you were, the more you earn.
